Conclusion and Recommendations

Question 17: What are your conclusions from the analysis? What recommendations would you like to share to help improve the business?

Conclusions:

Restaurant and Order Overview:

  • The FoodHub app hosts 178 restaurants, with a total of 1,898 orders placed.
  • Orders are more frequent on weekends compared to weekdays.
  • Shake shack, The Meatball shop, Blue Ribbon Sushi, Blue ribbon Fried Chicken have more than 50 ratings and its avg rating is more than 4.

Food Preparation and Delivery Time:

  • 50% of the orders are prepared in 27 minutes or less.
  • The average delivery time after food is prepared is 25 minutes, with most orders taking around 24 minutes.
  • On weekends, food delivery takes approximately 22 minutes, while on weekdays, it takes around 28 minutes.
  • Food delivery time does not solely depend on food preparation timefactors such as traffic may contribute to delays.
  • 10% of orders take more than 60 minutes to be delivered after being placed.

Order Cost and Spending Patterns:

  • Most orders fall within the dollar 12 to 15 range, while a few customers spend around dollar 30 to dollar 35.
  • 29% of orders cost more than dollar 20, meaning 71% of customers place orders under dollar 20.
  • French cuisine is the most expensive, with customers typically spending around dollar 30 to dollar 35.

Cuisine Popularity:

  • American cuisine is the most popular, receiving the highest number of orders,followed by Japanese,Italian and Chinese cuisine.
  • Vietnamese cuisine is the least ordered.

Rating:

  • The average food preparation time remains contant almost across different ratings.
  • Ratings do not seem to strongly influence food preparation time.
  • Orders with rating 5 ratings have the highest cost of order and ratings of 3 ratings show a slight dip.
  • Shorter and more consistent delivery times may lead to better ratings (rating 4).
  • Longer and more inconsistent delivery times tend to correlate with lower ratings (rating 3).

Recommendations:
